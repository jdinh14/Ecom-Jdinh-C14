Quality You Can Trust

When you source with Reagents, you can expect chemical manufacturing at the highest quality standards. Our facilities have the qualifications and certifications to meet most specifications, assuring you that we are dedicated to the quality of our products and our service.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility
  • Laboratory ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accredited testing facility
  • The Reagents' Laboratory ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accreditation covers the testing of pH, Conductivity, and Titration
  • Product labels that meet all OSHA's GHS requirements
  • NIST traceable standard reference material
  • Chemicals meet or exceed the ACS specifications as stated in Reagent Grade Chemicals, 11th Edition, American Chemical Society, 2016
  • Balances are calibrated against certified Class S weights
  • Class A volumetric glassware is calibrated as stated in ASTM E288 and NIST Circular 434
