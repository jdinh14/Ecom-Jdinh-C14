How to Register for a Web Account

Learn how to register for a web account at reagents.com. New customers will have a streamlined order experience, while existing customers can set up new users and access their company's products and pricing. Creating a web account with Reagents enhances your shopping experience, while still allowing you to have easy access to our Customer Service team.

How to Assign Customer Part Numbers

With a Reagents web account, you can assign customer part numbers to every item on our website. These numbers are only visible to you and other users in your company. They're even searchable and noted on your order. Now, once you know your product match, you can assign it YOUR part number on OUR website, saving you time and frustration! You can even bulk load part numbers by reaching out to customer service.

How to Assign Product Groups

With a Reagents web account, you can assign items to multiple product groups personalized to your orders and preferences. Streamline your repeat orders by having everything in one place and adding them to your cart with one easy click!

How to Use the Quick Order Pad

With a Reagents web account, you can quickly add multiple items to your cart with our quick order pad. You can add items to the list based on Manufacturer Part Number, Item Number, or Customer Part Number then add them all to your cart with one click!
