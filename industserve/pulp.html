Pulp and Paper

In the pulp and paper industry, downtime is expensive. Processes must run 24/7 and the testing lab has to keep pace. You depend on a trustworthy source of quality chemicals and timely delivery. Reagents chemicals are manufactured in an ISO 9001:2015 facility and tested in an ISO 17025 accredited laboratory. Reagents delivers on time to assure that deadlines are met and production continues without interruption. We offer a complete line of pre-made solutions freeing up lab staff to focus on critical work such as trouble-shooting, testing and research. From the chemistry of delignification to the bleaching of the paper, Reagents has the chemicals you need when you need them.

Top Chemicals are Often Used for:

  • ABC Testing
  • Ash & Moisture Testing
  • Alpha Cellulose Testing
  • Hercules Size Test
  • Kappa Numbers Test
  • Mineral Content Testing

  • Starch Test
