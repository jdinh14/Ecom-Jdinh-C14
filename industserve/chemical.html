Chemical

Reagents offers a wide variety of chemicals and solutions suited for laboratory testing of incoming raw materials, in process testing, and the testing of finished products. Our chemicals are produced under strict ISO guidelines for reliability and consistency. Reagents proudly holds both ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 17025 accreditations. We offer a wide range of products including HPLC solvents, mobile phases, buffers, acids, bases, ASTM testing solutions, and many more. We also distribute numerous brands and grades of production chemicals in 12kg, 200L, and Tote size packaging. Custom packaging of these chemicals can be done so your production team can use the whole contents of a package without weighing or having any excess product thus reducing exposure to hazardous chemicals. All Reagents brand products are labeled with date of manufacture or expiration dates. Product C of As and SDS sheets are available online 24/7.

Top Products are Commonly Used for:

  • Coulmetry
  • GC-MS & LC-MS Tests
  • HPLC Testing
  • Ion Chromatography
  • Karl Fischer Titration
